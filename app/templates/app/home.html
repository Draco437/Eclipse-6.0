ID — EC601

Distributed Resources

The Problem

Students often require high computing power for tasks like machine learning, simulations, and large-scale data processing, but personal laptops often lack the performance needed. Meanwhile, many CPUs and GPUs across campus remain idle for long periods. Design a system that allows students to share and access unused computing resources across a campus network, enabling efficient workload distribution.

SLM Voice Agents

The Problem

A Voice Agent allows users to interact with an AI through spoken conversation instead of text. The typical pipeline includes speech to text (STT) to convert audio into text, a language model that processes the request, and text-to-speech (TTS) that converts the response back into audio. Build a real-time Voice Agent powered by one or more fine-tuned Small Language Models (under ~7B parameters). Participants must use Oumi to fine-tune the models and build a solution that addresses a realistic use case.

ID — EC604

Knowledge Retrieval

The Problem

Modern organizations store large amounts of information across documents, databases, and internal tools, but this knowledge is often fragmented. Employees spend significant time searching across multiple platforms, leading to duplicated effort and missed insights. Design solutions that enable efficient discovery and retrieval of relevant knowledge across enterprise systems while maintaining context and grounding responses in reliable organizational data.

ID — EC605 Sponsored

Structured Testimony

The Problem

Survivors of sexual violence, torture, trafficking, and domestic abuse often face legal systems that require precise, linear accounts of traumatic events. Trauma frequently causes fragmented memory and inconsistent recall — responses that are clinically normal but often misinterpreted in court. Design solutions that help capture and structure trauma-related accounts in ways that respect the realities of trauma while supporting reliable legal documentation.

ID — EC606

Low-Bandwidth Learning

The Problem

Online education has grown rapidly, but many students in rural and low-income regions remain excluded due to unreliable internet connectivity. Most live-class platforms depend on continuous video streaming, which fails on slow networks or expensive plans. Create solutions that enable reliable participation in online education under low or unstable bandwidth, minimizing data usage and maintaining continuity during disruptions.

The Vibe-Audit

The Problem

AI-generated apps often look great but ignore security, PII compliance, and cost efficiency. Build an automated 'Production-Ready' gatekeeper for apps built via Vibe Coding that scans a repo and generates a Go/No-Go report covering hardcoded secrets, PII compliance (GDPR/SOC2), and hallucinated or insecure dependencies, ultimately delivering a 'Vibe-to-Value' score with auto-remediation prompts.

The SME-Plug

The Problem

General agents lack deep, niche expertise and often hallucinate when tasks get highly technical. Design a 'hot-swappable' universal SME plugin that injects specialized knowledge, specific reasoning logic, and decision trees into any agent (LangChain, AutoGPT, etc.) while enforcing 'Source of Truth' citations to provide a 'Plug-and-Play' expert module for any complex industry.
